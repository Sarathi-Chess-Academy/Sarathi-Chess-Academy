About Us

Welcome to Sarathi Chess Academy — Where Every Move Matters!

At Sarathi Chess Academy, we believe chess is more than just a game — it’s a journey of strategy, patience, and creativity. Founded with the mission to nurture young minds and empower passionate players, our academy provides structured training for all levels — from beginners discovering the beauty of chess to advanced players preparing for competitive excellence. With 200+ students trained and 50+ tournaments conducted, we are proud to be one of the fastest-growing chess academies dedicated to quality coaching and personal growth. Our experienced coaches focus on developing not only tactical and strategic thinking but also discipline, concentration, and decision-making skills that extend beyond the chessboard. Whether you’re taking your first step into chess or aiming for tournament mastery, Sarathi Chess Academy is your trusted partner in the game of kings.

Types of classes

Types of classes

Beginner Course

Sessions: 8 Classes/month

Who it’s for: This course is tailored For new learners starting out in chess—learning the rules, understanding how each piece moves, and building confidence in early play.

What you’ll learn: Basic moves, simple tactics (e.g. forks, pins), opening principles, basic endgames.

Intermediate

Beginner Course

Sessions: 8 Classes/month

Who it’s for: For those who know the basics well and want to improve their strategy, calculation, and positional understanding.

What you’ll learn: More in-depth tactics, opening repertoire, planning middlegame strategy, recognizing common patterns.

Advanced

Beginner Course

Sessions: 8 Classes/month

Who it’s for: Players ready to sharpen their skills seriously—aiming to compete, analyse games deeply, and build advanced thinking skills.

What you’ll learn: Advanced tactics, endgame mastery, advanced opening systems, game analysis, psychological and strategic depth.

Is there classes on both online and offline?

Yes we conduct both online and offline classes for all levels mentioned above.
